Avocado Toast & Eggs

Description

Yummy Yummy toast & eggs merged together with yummy yummy avocado rub.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 fresh avocado
  • 2 slices of wheat bread
  • 1/8 scoop of butter

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Prep eggs(Mix and beat down), rub butter on bread
  3. Using spoon, smooth down avocado to paste
  4. Fry egg on pan with 1/8 butter
  5. Toast bread on pan, flipping both sides till crunchy
  6. Butter avocado paste onto toast bread and layer with egg
  7. Season to taste- pepper and thyme
