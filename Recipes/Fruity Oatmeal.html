Fruity Oatmeal

Description

Yummy Yummy bunch-a-oatmeal infused with deliciously nutritious fruits blueberries, raspberries, bananas, strawberries and completely dipped in honey and crunchy nuts.

Ingredients

  • 5 strawberries
  • Two bananas
  • One cup of honey
  • One cup of blueberries
  • 2 cups of steel-cut oats
